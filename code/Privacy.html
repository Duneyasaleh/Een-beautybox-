Privacy

**Your Privacy and Safety **

Beauty box - Member of the make up group assumes responsibility for careful handling of your personal details. On this page we provide information about how your data are gathered when you visit our website. If you still have queries, please do not hesitate to contact our Customer Services.

Gathered Data
This website uses the gathered data for the following purposes:

1- placing an order on our website we need your name, email address, telephone number, delivery address, and payment details. This information is necessary to process your order.
2- You have the opportunity to set up an account with us. The above details are kept and stored with your account within a safe environment. This avoids you having to enter your details again for every new order.
3- For sending notification of special offers and actions regarding our products. If you wish to cancel your subscription to the newsletter you can do so by contacting our Customer Services:

No Sale to Third Parties
Beauty box will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your details will be passed on only to those responsible for carrying out your order and payment.

Changes in your Details
You have the absolute right to view and to change your details. For this purpose, please contact our Customer Service.
